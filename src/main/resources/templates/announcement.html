CFL - Team Players Substitution

Dear Team Owners,

We now announce the second team players SUBSTITUTION in CFL. 'Modify Team' option will be enabled as per below timings :-

Offshore :- June 13th (06:00 AM IST) to June 13th (11:30 PM IST)

Onshore :- June 12th (07:30 PM CST) to June 13th (01:00 PM CST)

Rules for team players substitution are as follows:

  • Each team owner can substitute 2 of their players with new players, however substitution should be done with same skill and overall same $ value.
  • Player's substitution is allowed only during the defined period.
  • You will not loose any point for the replaced players and new players will start earning from their future matches only.

Happy Playing !!!
