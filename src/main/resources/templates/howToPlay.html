Rules

  • This contest is open for Walgreens employee and consultant only.
  • User should register to the site with their Walgreens email ID only.
  • Each player has to create the team of 11 players.
  • Each team should have a name given by team owner.
  • Participation fee for this game is $5.00 (Onshore) and Rs 200 (Offshore). We are collecting fee to ensure serious participation and end of the game total money will be distributed amongst top 3 players.
  • Each player has assigned a $ value based individual skill & their ICC ranking.
  • Each team owner can use max $21 (virtual money) to create the team of 11 players.
  • While creating the team each team owner should follow either one of the below skill set. Set #1 : 5Bat+5Bwl+1wk , Set #2 : 4Bat+4Bwl+2All+1wk
  • Each team owner can substitute a maximum 2 of their incumbent players with new players each time however substitution should be done with same skill & $ value players.
  • Unlimited substitution will be allowed till 29th May 11.59 P.M. IST.
  • Players substitution (After 29th May 11.59 P.M) will be allowed twice during entire tournament schedule and substitution window time will be communicated by system administrator.
  • New player points will not roll over to the team.
  • You will not loose any point for the replaced players and new players will start earning from their future matches only.
  • To win the game, individual team must work towards attaining the maximum points.
  • In case if tie amount will be equally distributed among winners.
  • Any rule can be changed by system administrator without prior notice.
  • In case of any dispute system administrator decision will be final and abide.

Points calculation

  • 1 point per run scored
  • 2 bonus points per boundary hit (i.e.4 runs)
  • 4 bonus points per six runs scored
  • 20 bonus points on half-century
  • 20 bonus point on a century
  • 20 points per wicket
  • 25 bonus points on 3 wicket haul
