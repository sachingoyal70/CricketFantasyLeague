Rules

  • This contest is only open via referral from the admin team. The referred person can invite their friends and family members.
  • Each player has to create the team of 11 players.
  • Each team should have a name given by team owner.
  • We'll be charging a minimal amount of ₹ 50 to cover our server,api and domain cost. This amount need to be paid by the team owner during team activation.
  • Each player has been assigned a $ value based on individual skill & parameters decided by admin team.
  • Each team owner can use max $21 (virtual money) to create the team of 11 players.
  • While creating the team each team owner should follow either one of the below skill set. Set #1 : 5Bat+5Bwl+1wk Set #2 : 4Bat+4Bwl+2All+1wk. The above set will be deemed final and will not be allowed to change during the course of the CFL 2020 season.
  • Unlimited player substitution will be allowed before the 1st match of CFL 2020 (19th Sep 6 PM IST).
  • No player substitutions will be allowed during the 1st week of CFL 2020 season. (19th Sep'2020 till 25th Sep'2020).
  • After 25th Sep each team owner can substitute a maximum of 50 players during the course of the CFL 2020 season. The substitution should be done with same skill & $ value players. .
  • Players substitution window will be allowed from (26th Sep 2020) between 3 AM IST to 3 PM IST daily. A team owner can substitute any number of players from his/her team. However, the total count of substituted players cannot go beyond 50 players during the course of the CFL 2020 season.
  • New player points will not roll over to the team.
  • You will not loose any point for the replaced players and new players will start earning from their future matches only.
  • To win the game, individual team must work towards attaining the maximum points.
  • Any rule can be changed by system administrator without prior notice.
  • In case of any dispute system administrator decision will be final and abide.

Points calculation

  • 1 point per run scored
  • 1 points per boundary hit (i.e.4 runs)
  • 2 points per six runs scored
  • 20 points per wicket
  • 20 bonus points on each 50 Run
  • 25 bonus points each 3 wicket
  • 1 bonus point to each dot ball by bowler
  • 20 bonus point to each maiden over
